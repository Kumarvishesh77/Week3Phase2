SkillBridge Overview

Platform Purpose

SkillBridge helps you understand your current skill readiness and provides clear guidance toward your target role.

How it Works

  • Profile Creation: Capture your background, skills, and career goals.
  • Skill Assessment: Measure your current proficiency through structured evaluations.
  • Skill Gap Analysis: Identify strengths, improvement areas, and missing skills for your target role.
  • Personalized Roadmap: Follow a prioritized learning path to achieve your career objectives.

User Benefits

  • Clear understanding of your skill readiness
  • Visibility into strengths and improvement areas
  • Role‑based skill gap insights
  • Actionable guidance for learning and growth

Students Beginners Professionals Career Switchers
